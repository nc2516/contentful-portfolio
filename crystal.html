Personal Crystal

Instead of those personality quizzes that have around 4 to 6 possible outcomes, I wanted to create a more personalized personality quiz for people to see which crystal suits them. I coded it with HTML/CSS/Javascript to create 5 questions with 2 answers each, with each combination getting a different crystal, resulting in 32 possibilities.

The questions are very specific and a large magical, surrealistic, and dream-like situation.
