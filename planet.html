Planet Exploration

Planet Exploration is a website for learning new things about each of the planets. I coded it with HTML/CSS/Javascript and all assets are illustrated by me on Procreate. As you click on each of the planets, you will learn the planet's diameter, distance from the sun, planet type, surface temperature, and a fun fact.There is also a secret easter egg surprise when you click one of the stars which will let you "name a star or make a wish", and you can see what others have wished.
