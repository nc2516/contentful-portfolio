Zodiac Filter p5.js

Zodiac is a p5.js project in collaboration with Bailey Foltz. Together, we created this filter that lets you input your birthday to learn which zodiac sign you are, and you are given a fortune. Then you can see yourself with a filter that correlates with your zodiac. All zodiac filters drawn by me on procreate.
